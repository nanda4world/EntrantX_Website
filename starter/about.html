Our 6D Process

Our 6D process assures you to choose us in taking your brand to its next level.

Discover

We seek to understand where you are and where you wish to go. We take time to understand your marketing goals and objectives.

Define

We start out by defining the necessary requirements for your project. Specific deliverables are agreed upon & the web strategy is put in place.

Design

After the goals, specifications and requirements have been assessed we move into designing the Website’s architecture.

Develop

In the development stage, the finalized design files are handed off to the development team to implement the code and back-end systems to make the Website interactive.

Deploy
